Parallel Archive

In the 11th arrondissement of Paris, Parallel Archive brings together independent young artists from around the world, with a pulse centered on Paris and Berlin.

Founded by a collector devoted to modern fashion culture, provocative aesthetics, and future-facing forms, the museum is both an exhibition space and a platform. We present and sell bold contemporary works that explore sensuality, movement, and new identities in urban life.

Independent. Young. Futuristic. Modern.
